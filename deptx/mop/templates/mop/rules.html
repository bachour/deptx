Tasks

Your job is it to finish tasks.
You find the available tasks under "Tasks".
Depending on your trust level there will be more tasks available for you.
If you want to work on a task, note down its task identification number.
Fill in all necessary data into the appropriate form (depending on the MOP unit handing out the task)
Compose a new message.
Send the message to the appropriate unit
Select "Sending Form" as the subject
Attach the appropriate form to the message.
Send the message.
After a while you should get a notification message.
If everything was correct with your form, the task will now be assigned to you.
Go back to "Tasks" and start working!
